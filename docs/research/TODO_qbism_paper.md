## Core Thesis
QBism's agent-centric quantum epistemology and PCI's G₂-geometric consciousness framework share deep structural parallels. Both elevate coherence to a foundational role, both reject objective collapse, both center the observer — but QBism operates epistemically (beliefs about outcomes) while PCI operates ontologically (geometric structure of the observer). The G₂ formalism provides the mathematical backbone that the original PCI–QBism comparison (2025) was missing.

## Key Arguments to Develop
1. **Dutch-book coherence ↔ Torsion-free convergence**: QBism says violating Born rule = sure loss (Dutch book). PCI says violating coherence = torsion blow-up (Type I singularity). Both are consequences of breaking coherence — one probabilistic, one geometric.
2. **Born rule as normative ↔ Born rule as emergent from G₂ structure**: QBism says Born rule is a constraint on rational belief. PCI says the 6/7 coherence ceiling and spectral structure constrain the dynamics. Both derive constraints from structure (Hilbert space dimension vs. G₂ algebra dimension).
3. **Agent-relative facts ↔ Observer-relative torsion classes**: QBism says facts are personal until communicated. PCI says each observer has their own torsion decomposition T₁⊕T₇⊕T₁₄⊕T₂₇. Different observers = different torsion histories = different coherence states.
4. **SIC-POVMs and G₂ geometry**: Fuchs' SIC conjecture involves structures in d²-dimensional space. G₂ acts on 7-dimensional space with 49 = 7² torsion components. Investigate whether there's a deeper connection between SIC dimension (d²) and torsion dimension (7²).
5. **Retrocausality**: QBism is agnostic on retrocausality. PCI's Laplacian flow convergence to torsion-free fixed points is formally equivalent to future states attracting present dynamics. Explore whether QBism's "belief update" can be reframed as convergence in a coherence space.
6. **Wigner's friend**: QBism dissolves it (agent-relative facts). PCI predicts it (paradox as ontological engine → singularity → reorganization). The G₂ singularity taxonomy gives PCI a classification of *what kind* of paradox resolution occurs.
7. **Ward (2026) bridge**: Ward's constraint-closure for NDA maps onto both QBism (precarious constraints as minimal belief structures) and PCI (div T = 0 as minimal torsion equilibrium). Three-way comparison.
