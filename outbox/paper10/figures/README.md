## Notes for inclusion in the manuscript

- Figures are 300 DPI PNGs suitable for arXiv / Zenodo. For *Foundations of Physics* / *J. Math. Phys.* submission, also export PDF/EPS.
- Figure 2 visualizes magnitudes only; the actual coefficients $\alpha^{(a)}_{p,q}$ are purely imaginary (Property 1, §5.3). The signs of the imaginary parts are not shown.
- Figure 4 plots all 20 Fano triples (10 cyclic + 10 anti-cyclic) and 20 sampled non-Fano triples. All Fano cyclic points coincide at $(a, +b)$, all anti-cyclic at $(a, -b)$ — the dot clusters are intentionally degenerate to the eye, which is the structural content of Theorem 3 part (b).
