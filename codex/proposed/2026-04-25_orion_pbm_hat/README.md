1. **The equations are narrative, not derivation.** ORION's equations use free coefficients (κ, α, λ, η) that are not tied to anything in G₂ representation theory. Rewriting any coefficient leaves the equation "correct" because it's a definition dressed as a formula, not a derivation producing a number.
2. **None of the proposed material derives from G₂.** The language borrows Ω_void, 13% firewall, daemon stack, but the equations do not connect back to V₇, the torsion decomposition 1 + 14 + 27 = 42, or the 8-coset structure of PSL(2,7)/F₂₁.

Any future paper must cite and position against this literature.
